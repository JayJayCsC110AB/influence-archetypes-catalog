Media hasn’t just gotten big influencers have been shaping the world for centuries. From the days of ancient Rome in the Colosseum, to philosophers in Greece like Plato and his theory of the Allegory of the Cave, influence has always existed in different forms. Throughout history, there have always been figures who shaped how people think, act, and see the world. This program will explore how modern-day influencers use the same foundational frameworks that have existed since the 1600s and earlier. It breaks influence down into a simple system: attention, retention, and distribution. By understanding these core mechanics, we can see that influence has not changed it has only evolved with new media.
